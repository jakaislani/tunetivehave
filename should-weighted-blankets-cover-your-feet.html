Should a weighted blanket cover your whole body?

At a minimum, a weighted blanket that you are going to use nightly should cover your body from the neck down, without a lot left over. Since they tend to keep you still during the night, unless you're a restless sleeper you don't need the blanket to be so big that it covers the whole bed.
