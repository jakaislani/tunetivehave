Bindi Irwin is one of the well-known names all over the entertainment sector. Irwin is an Australian/American television host, actress, singer, and the conservationist widely popular as the host of children's television nature documentary series, 'Bindi the Jungle Girl.'

Bindi Sue Irwin was born on July 24, 1998, in Buderim, Queensland, Australia, to the renowned parent's late. Steve Irwin (father) and Terri Irwin (mother). Bindi has a youngest brother named Robert Irwin. Like Bindi, Robert is also a TV star.

Celebrity Parents: Steve & Terri

Since childhood Bindi is living a limelight life and is also regarded as the celebrity daughter. Her father late. Steve Irwin aka The Crocodile Hunter was an Austrian conservationist, TV personality, and wildlife expert whose prominence rises from the TV series The Crocodile Hunter.

On the other hand, Bindi's mother Terri Irwin also a conservationist, TV personality. Terri is also the owner of the Australia Zoo located in Beerwah, Queensland. Moreover, she is also an author who has published books like My Steve, Crocs & Gators, etc. Terri and Steve first met back in 1991. 

After the very first encounter, they shared a good bond and later tied the wedding knot in 1992. From the marital bond, they shared two kids and were parenting them jointly. Everything was going fine. However, after the tragic death of Steve, the happiness of the family shattered. Steve died on September 4, 2006, after being pierced in the chest by a stingray (a fish related to sharks).

Who is Bindi Irwin's Husband?

The 22-years-old Bindi is in a marital relationship with Chandler Powell. Irwin's husband Chandler is the professional wakeboarder. Powell currently serves at the Australia Zoo alongside his wife Bindi.

Powell first met Irwin back in 2013 while he was visiting the Australia zoo with his parents. Afterward, the Bindi and Chandler remain close friends for more than a year. Afterwar, in 2015, they officially began their romantic affair.

Then the beautiful couple merrily shared their engagement ring in July 2019 nearly after four years long dating. The lovebird revealed the news of their engagement via Instagram post.

Private Wedding Ceremony

After the happiest engagement, without any delay the power couple Bindi & Chandler tied the wedding knot on March 25, 2020, at the Australia Zoo. The marriage ceremony took place privately in front of the family members only. They did not invite any guests due to coronavirus pandemic.

On the big day, the bride Bindi was looking gorgeous on her full sleeve wedding gown designed by Paddington Weddings (Bertossi Brides Wedding Dress). On the other hand, the groom Chandler dashes the look wearing a blue shirt and light brown pants.

As of the newly married pair is living a blissful life. They prefer to spend their quality time together and are sharing a good bond as a perfect married pair. Furthermore, they also never miss a chance to flaunt romantic pictures on their social accounts.

Net Worth: $3 million

The media personality Bindi has a net worth of $3 million which she collected from her professional career. The television personality has appeared in TV programs like Wiggly Safari, Bindi's Bootcamp, Steve Irwin's Wildlife Warrior, Curious George, and Have you been paying attention?

As of now, Bindi is working in the American reality series Crickey? It's the Irwins alongside her mother and brother. Similarly, she has performed in films such as The Crocodile Hunter: Collision Course (2002), Free Willy: Escape from Pirate's Cove (2010), and Return to Nim's Island (2013).

Besides these all, Bindi is also broadly popular as the winner of season 21 of the dance competition series Dancing with the Stars (U.S). There her dance partner was Derek Hough.

Is Also A Singer

The 5-feet-4-inch (150 cm) tall Bindi is also a singer who has released albums like Bindi Kid Fitness with Steve Irwin and The Crocmen (2006), Bindi Kid Fitness 2 Jungle Dance Party (2008), and others.

Irwin who was nominated for ARIA Music Awards is also appeared in the album Wiggly Safari by the Australian Children's music group, 'The Wiggles.' Additionally, concerning the awards, Bindi has received Logie Awards, Kid Choice Awards, and Daytime Emmy Award. 

Irwin was also awarded the Young Conservationist of the Year by the Australian Geographic Society.
