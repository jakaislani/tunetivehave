Blake Wheeler is an American professional ice hockey player who plays for the Winnipeg Jets of the National Hockey League (NHL). He is also the caption of the Jets team.

Besides, Wheeler plays as a right-wing and wears jersey number 26. Before coming into his current team, he played for different teams. He signed an entry-level contract with the Boston Bruins on July 1, 2008.

And he was named to the 2009 NHL YoungStars Game to represent the rookies, where Blake earned Game MVP honors after a four-goal effort to beat the sophomores 9–5.

Additionally, Boston traded Wheeler to the Atlanta Thrashers on February 18, 2011, along with Mark Stuart, for Boris Valábik and Rich Peverley.
