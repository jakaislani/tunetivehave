Emma Hernan first joined the Selling Sunset cast in season 4 and has become a strong character ever since despite her epic fall our with former star Christine Quinn.

Who is Emma Hernan? What's her age and background?

Emma, 31, was born in Boston, Massachusetts and celebrates her birthday on December 29th.

As well as working as a real estate agent, Emma is an entrepreneur - and has her own frozen food company called Emma Leigh & Co.

Emma recently posted about her excitement for the start of the show, writing: "May 19th!!! buckle up for safety!!"

Emma Hernan's swimming career

In episode three of Selling Sunset in season 4, Emma revealed that she is a swimmer and previously qualified for the olympics.

She told property developer Micah McDonald that she specialised in backstroke while she was showing him round a house.

Emma previously revealed that she gave up swimming when she was 16 to focus on modelling in Paris.
