Samuel L Jackson Net Worth & Income

As a result of his 40 plus years in the industry and countless hit movies, TV shows, and productions, Samuel L Jackson has amassed a staggering net worth of $250 million.

Even though almost all of his income comes from his acting career, he also makes good money from brand endorsements and other business pursuits.

To date, he has starred in more than 150 films.

He makes anywhere between $10 and $20 million for a single starring role in a major production.

Similarly, one of the most admired characters of Nick Fury earns him $4 to $6 million every time he appears in a Marvel movie, even if it is just a cameo scene with two lines.

Moreover, he made a mere $75,000 from 1992’s crime thriller film ‘White Sands‘.

Likewise, the 2000’s action crime film Shaft brought him $10 million, and Unbreakable earned him $7 million.

So basically, we can say that he can earn up to $20 million when backend bonuses are included.

Samuel L. Jackson | Movies and Endorsements

Movies

Samuel L. Jackson started his career appearing in minor roles in various TV films before moving to New York from Atlanta in 1976.

The next decade he would only appear in stage productions.

After his performance in A Soldier’s Play in 1981, he met director Spike Lee. Lee later went on to cast him for minor roles in his films.

It was this time around when Mr. Jackson also struggled with drugs addiction.

In the early 1990s, Sam appeared in numerous big-budget production, including 1990 Goodfellas, 1991 Jungle Fever, 1993 Jurassic Park, and others.

And in 1994, he played his breakthrough role of Jules Winnfield in Quentin Tarantino’s Pulp Fiction. Jackson was nominated for Academy Awards for his role in the same.

Then, he landed in admirable roles in Stars Wars and MCU.

Also, he has also been a part of several animated movies and shows as a voice actor.

Moreover, as of now, his schedule is jam-packed with upcoming projects, some of which is still filming, and some are in post-production.

Samuel L. Jackson | Career

Samuel L. Jackson was born in Washington D.C., United States, on 21st December 1948.

He was born to Roy and Elizabeth but was raised only by his mother in Chattanooga, Tennessee.

Sam obtained a high school degree from Riverside High School. After that, he enrolled in Morehouse College, located in Atlanta, Georgia.

He made his film debut with the 1972 independent film Together for Days.

Furthermore, in 1980 Jackson married producer LaTanya Richardson whom he met while attending college in Atlanta.

Together they have a daughter Zoe who was born in 1982.

Besides, his DNA test traced part of his ancestry back to the Benga people of Gabon.

He became a citizen of Gabon in the year 2019.
