Daniela Ruah Age

Daniela Ruah was born on December 2, 1983, in Boston, Massachusetts, to Portuguese parents.

She is currently 39 years old.

Daniela moved back to Portugal with her family when she was five years old and grew up there.

She started acting at the age of 16 and won a Portuguese dance competition at 18.

Daniela then moved to London to study performing arts at the London Metropolitan University.

She later moved to New York to study at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute before landing her role on NCIS: Los Angeles in 2009.

Daniela Ruah Eye Condition

One of the most distinctive features of Daniela Ruah is her right eye, which has a darker color than her left eye.

This is due to a birthmark called nevus of Ota, which affects the pigmentation of the eye and the surrounding skin.

Daniela has embraced her unique eye and considers it her trademark.

She has said that it does not affect her vision or her career and that she hates it when it is Photoshopped or edited out.

She has also become an inspiration for others who have similar conditions and has encouraged them to be proud of their differences.

Daniela Ruah Spouse

Daniela Ruah is married to David Paul Olsen, who is also an actor and a stunt performer.

He is the older brother and stunt double of Eric Christian Olsen, who plays Marty Deeks on NCIS: Los Angeles.

The couple met on the set of the show, thanks to Eric’s matchmaking efforts.

Eric noticed that both Daniela and David were unhappy in their previous relationships and decided to introduce them to each other.

He said that they were both amazing people who deserved to be happy.

His plan worked, as Daniela and David hit it off and started dating in 2011.

How Many Children Does Daniela Ruah Have?

Daniela Ruah and David Paul Olsen have two children together: a son named River Isaac, who was born in December 2013, and a daughter named Sierra Esther, who was born in September 2016.

Daniela has shared many adorable photos and videos of her kids on her Instagram account, where she has over 1.5 million followers.

She has also revealed that she maintained her work on NCIS: Los Angeles throughout both of her pregnancies, with some adjustments made by the writers and the crew.

Daniela Ruah’s Net Worth
