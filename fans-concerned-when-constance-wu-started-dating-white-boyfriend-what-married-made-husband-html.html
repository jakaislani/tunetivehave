Crazy Rich Asians actress Constance Wu kept her pregnancy out of the public eye and mentioned her nine-month-old child for the first time at The Tonight Show, Starring Jimmy Fallon.

Baby Girl With A Blue Butt 

During The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon’s episode of May 21, 2021, when Jimmy questioned Constance about her baby girl, she responded by saying- 

 “She’s the best! She has a full head of hair, she has a blue butt.”

While talking about her baby’s blue butt, Wu added- 

“Yes, her butt is the color blue...There’s this thing, I had never heard of it before, but it’s called a ‘Mongolian spot.’ And apparently, it happens in a lot of Asian babies, and my boyfriend and I are both Asian, and it’s where your butt is blue for the first two years of your life and then it just goes away.”

Constance and her boyfriend Ryan Kattner welcomed their baby girl on the summer of 2020 and hid the news regarding the pregnancy from the public eye. 

Kattner is a musician who is famous for his stage name Honus Honus. He is a frontman of the rock band Man Man. As Wu likes to keep her personal life under wraps, she has not opened up about her relationship with her boyfriend.

Criticism For Dating A White Man 

While dating her ex-partner, a white man Ben Hethcoat, Wu faced a lot of backlashes.  

After she criticized the whitewashing of the great Chinese history with the casting of actor Matt Damon in 2017's The Great Wall, Constance received a lot of criticism and hatred as she was dating Ben Hethcoat-  a director and producer at the time. 

Constance Wu with boyfriend Ben Hethcoat. (Photo: yomyomf.com)

Several People expressed their thoughts on this topic after her comment. 

Initially, Wu and her then-boyfriend Ben met back in 2011 during a Christmas Party held by Ryan Spindell.

The couple were together for almost four years and decided to split. However, they did appear together back in January 2016 during the 21st Annual Critics Choice Awards held at Barker Hanger Airport in Los Angeles. Further, neither Constance nor Ben made any statement regarding their breakup.

More About Constance Wu 

Golden Glode nominated actress Constance Wu was born in Richmond, Virginia, to Fang-Sheng Wu- a professor at Virginia Commonwealth University and a computer programmer mother. 

She resides in Los Angeles with her pet rabbit, Lida Rose.

Wu made her first appearance as a supporting character in Stephanie Daley, and she is best known for her appearance in Crazy Rich Asians and Hustlers.
