Kylian Mbappe: Career

Mbappe started his career at Bondy. Initially, he learned the skills under his father, Wilfried.

Later, he moved to the local academy named Clairefontaine and performed outstandingly. Hence, Kylian got offers from big clubs across Europe.

However, he decided to settle down in Monaco, France.

To begin with, he got a call up to the Monaco B team in 2015 by Leonardo Jardim. However, Kylian’s skills and maturity forced the club to promote him to the first team.

Meanwhile, he debuted for the Monaco first team against Caen in Ligue 1 and became the youngest Monaco player to play for the first team.

Likewise, Mbappe scored his first goal against Troyes at 17 and registered him as the youngest Monaco player to score for the first team.

But, the breakthrough came out for him in the 2016-17 season when Kylian won his first Ligue 1 title. In the process, he scored his first hattrick against Rennes.

He played 29 matches in Ligue 1  in the 2016-17 season and scored 15 vital goals.

The soccer player joined PSG for a record transfer fee from rival club Monaco in 2017. Meanwhile, Kylian continued his brilliant form and won Ligue 1 Player of the year for the 2018-19 season.
